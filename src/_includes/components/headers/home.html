Ticha ↗ (pronounced [ˈti.tʃə] from the Colonial Valley Zapotec word for 'word,' 'language,' or 'text') is an international effort to document and promote Zapotec languages and knowledge through workshops, events, and online resources provided to the public at no cost.

Here in the Ticha Digital Text Explorer, you'll find Zapotec documents collected across archives as well as Spanish translation documents to study and compare. Some documents are provided as Digital Editions with additonal transcription, translation, and linguistic analysis work.
